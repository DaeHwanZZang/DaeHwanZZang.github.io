Ghost Book Store

Debut | April 24, 2024, Single <Children with the Blood of Stars>

Ghost Book Store is a South Korean indie band known for their dreamy sound and emotional lyrics. They debuted on April 24, 2024, with the single <Children with the Blood of Stars>, showcasing music that spans various genres such as dream pop, rock, and electronic. The members create a unique atmosphere and lyrical melodies based on their individual musical styles, singing about youth, imagination, and the fantasies within everyday life.

Erotic Worms Exhibition

Debut | November 2, 2023, EP <Anatomy>

An indie pop band reminiscent of pre-sleep imaginations, wrapped in love and hate, happiness and sorrow, shame and confident smiles. Composed of Hyunwoo Ahn (songwriting and guitar), Yeji Kim (bassist), Sangwon Ahn (drummer), and Anidonno (vocalist), this is a four-member group based in Busan.

Shin In Ryu

Debut | September 26, 2018, Single <Your Words>

Shin Inryu is a South Korean indie band formed in 2018, gaining attention for their emotional melodies and sincere lyrics. The band consists of members Shin Onyu (vocal), Moon Junghwan (bass), and Ha Hyungeon (keyboard). Starting with their debut single <Your Words>, they have continued to perform and release music, delicately portraying stories of everyday life and youth. Their music is characterized by a dreamy yet warm sound, and they consistently present new musical experiments.

TRPP

Chi-Chi Cliché, a Chinese-French member who has lived a nomadic life without a home. Furukawa Yukio, a Japanese rock enthusiast who gave up inheriting a three-generation ramen shop and started living in Seoul out of love for Korea. Bye Bye Badman

Debut | January 7, 2011, EP <Bye Bye Badman>

Bye Bye Badman is a South Korean indie rock band formed in 2011. They are known for their sophisticated sound and stylish melodies, based on various genres such as Britpop, shoegazing, and alternative rock. Starting with their debut EP <Bye Bye Badman>, they have released several albums, including <Light Beside You> and <Authentic>. With emotional lyrics and a dreamy atmosphere, they have garnered a large fan base. The band consists of three members: Jung Bong-gil (main vocal, guitar), Lee Ruri (bass), and Cloud (keyboard, chorus).
